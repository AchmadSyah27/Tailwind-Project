About Kopiko

We're passionate about sharing the perfect cup of coffee with our community through quality, sustainability, and exceptional service.

Our History, Mission & Values

Founded in 2010, Kopiko started as a small coffee cart with a big dream. Our mission is to provide exceptional coffee experiences while supporting sustainable farming practices and building community connections. We value quality, authenticity, and creating welcoming spaces where everyone feels at home.
